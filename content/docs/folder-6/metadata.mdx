## SEO Metadata Generator (Beta)
Welcome to the **BloxAI SEO Metadata Generator**! This tool is designed to help you create SEO-friendly metadata for your webpages, improving their visibility in search engines.

<Accordions>
<Accordion title="How it Works">
1. **Enter Page Description**: This field allows you to specify the details of your webpage. Provide a concise and clear description to generate accurate metadata.

2. **Generate Metadata**: Click the "Generate Metadata" button to produce SEO-friendly metadata (title, description, keywords) based on your page description.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Tips for Effective SEO Metadata">
- **Be Descriptive**: Use clear and descriptive language to accurately represent the content of your webpage.
- **Include Keywords**: Incorporate relevant keywords that users are likely to search for.
- **Keep it Concise**: Metadata should be concise yet informative, keeping within recommended character limits.
- **Unique Titles and Descriptions**: Ensure each page has unique titles and descriptions to avoid duplication.
- **Use Action Words**: Use action-oriented language to encourage clicks from search results.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Example">
**Page Description**: 
- An e-commerce page selling the latest smartphones, featuring detailed product descriptions, reviews, and pricing information.

**Generated Metadata**:
```html
<!-- Title -->
<title>Buy the Latest Smartphones - Reviews, Prices, and More</title>

<!-- Meta Description -->
<meta name="description" content="Explore our selection of the latest smartphones with detailed reviews, competitive prices, and customer ratings. Shop now for the best deals on top brands.">

<!-- Meta Keywords -->
<meta name="keywords" content="latest smartphones, smartphone reviews, buy smartphones online, smartphone prices, best smartphone deals">
```
</Accordion>
