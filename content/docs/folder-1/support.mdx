Curious about supporting a new-age design tool like BloxUI? It might seem unusual, but we believe that every great community starts with passionate early adopters.

Rest assured, BloxUI remains free and accessible for all. But, for those who truly believe in the vision, becoming an early supporter gives you something extra. Our supporters are more than just users—they’re the heart of our growth, our VIPs.

## Perks for Early Supporters

- **Lifetime Access**: Get into our exclusive Early Bird Discord room, forever.
- **Exclusive Invitations**: Receive invites to special events, including exclusive meet-ups and design workshops.
- **Swag and Goodies**: Receive care packages with cool BloxUI swag.
- **Locked-in Pricing**: Enjoy fixed pricing, no matter how we evolve our offerings in the future.
- **First Look**: Be the first to try out new features as they roll out.
- **And More**: We’ll keep adding perks as we continue to grow together.

Your support fuels BloxUI's growth and evolution. Join us on this exciting journey!

## How to Support Us

Simply give us a Star on GitHub, and you’re in!
